GURU

Horticulture Expert 360°

Guru is an award-winning Landscaping firm which is specialized in Landscape Design, Execution and AMC Services with passion for design and a drive for excellence. The Team comprises of members having distinction in various fields such as Landscape Architects, Horticulturists, Civil Engineers, Electrical and Mechanical Engineers, Draughtsman, Project managers, Quality Assurance & Quality Control Managers, Planning and Co-ordination Managers etc, such that our works for clients goes as a comprehensive package with total solutions and services for the landscape needs. With over 25 years of experience in landscape design and project development, Guru has developed a celebrated reputation in the field of landscape industry and a long list of satisfied clients. Our Landscape Design & Development practice is well known for its high-quality detailing and customized landscape design solutions especially for Natural Environments, Corporate Spaces, Campus Developments, Green field Projects, Children’s Park, Sensory Gardens, Hospitality, Industrial and Institutional spaces and other areas in public domain.
Our mission at Guru is to make a positive difference in the quality of life. It’s about the artistry of blending our client’s vision with architecture and nature.
Every client and property has a unique expression and we strive to find the balance & bring them to life.
